#### What I care about

- **Privacy by architecture**, not by policy. If your data never leaves your machine, there's nothing to leak.
- **Offline-first**. The best apps work on a plane, on an island, in a basement with no signal.
- **Open weights > closed APIs**. The future of AI is local, personal, and owned by the user.
