### 💡 What I believe in

🔒 **Privacy by architecture** — if your data never leaves your machine, there's nothing to leak

📡 **Offline-first** — the best apps work on a plane, on an island, in a basement with no signal

🔓 **Open weights > closed APIs** — AI should be local, personal, and owned by the user
